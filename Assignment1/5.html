My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Shaolin Soccer
    • Year Released: 2001
    • Main Actors: Stephen Chow, Zhao Wei
    • Description: A Shaolin follower assembles his five childhood friends so that they can use their martial arts skills to play football and thereby popularize Shaolin kung fu.
  2. Iron Mask
    • Year Released: 2019
    • Main Actors: Jackie Chan, Arnold Schwarzenegger, Jason Flemyng
    • Description: Early 1700: Cartographer Jonathan Green (Jason Flemyng) from Forbidden Empire (2014) is back to map the Russian Far East. He's forced on to China, where he confronts the Dragon Master et al. The iron masked Russian Czar escapes the Tower of London to a Russian ship.
  3. The Karate Kid
    • Year Released: 2010
    • Main Actors: Jaden Smith, Wenwen Han, Jackie Chan
    • Description: Dre, a young boy, finds himself bullied by Cheng, a rebellious kung fu prodigy at his school. However, things change after he befriends Mr Han, a maintenance man, who happens to be a kung fu expert.
  4. Aladdin
    • Year Released: 2019
    • Main Actors: Mena Massoud, Naomi Scott, Will Smith
    • Description: Aladdin, a kind thief, woos Jasmine, the princess of Agrabah, with the help of Genie. When Jafar, the grand vizier, tries to usurp the king, Jasmine, Aladdin and Genie must stop him from succeeding..
  5. Wish
    • Year Released: 2023
    • Main Actors: Chris Pine, Ariana DeBose
    • Description: "Wish" is an all-new musical-comedy welcoming audiences to the magical kingdom of Rosas, where Asha, a sharp-witted idealist, makes a wish so powerful that it is answered by a cosmic force--a little ball of boundless energy called Star.
